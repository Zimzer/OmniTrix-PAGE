Privacy Policy for OmniTrix Discord Bot

This Privacy Policy describes how zimzer_ ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of the OmniTrix Discord bot ("the Bot"). Your privacy is critically important to us. We are committed to protecting it and being transparent about the data we process. By using the Bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. What Data Do We Collect?

We only collect data that is absolutely necessary for the Bot to function properly, to provide its features, and to improve our services.

Information Provided by Discord

  • ⬩ User ID: Your unique Discord identification number. We use this to save your individual settings and identify you within our system.
  • ⬩ Server ID: The unique identification number for the server the Bot is on. We use this to save server-specific settings.
  • ⬩ Channel ID: The unique identification number for the channel where a command is used. We use this for logging and channel-specific features.

Information Collected Automatically:

  • ⬩ Command Logs: We store information about the commands used, including the command content, timestamp, User ID, and Server ID. This is used for analytics, debugging, and abuse prevention.

Information Provided by You:

  • ⬩ Configuration and Settings: We store the settings you configure for the Bot on your server (e.g., prefix, module settings).
  • ⬩ Data Required for Command Functionality: Some commands may require you to provide content (e.g., text for a reminder), which will be stored temporarily for the command to function.

What We DO NOT Collect:

  • ⬩ We do not store message content that is not directly aimed at the Bot as a command. The Bot can see message content to detect commands, but it does not save these messages to a database.
  • ⬩ We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) like your email address, Discord password, age, or gender.

2. How Do We Use Your Data?

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • ⬩ To Provide the Bot's Functionality: Data like Server IDs and User IDs are essential for the Bot to offer personalized settings and features.
  • ⬩ To Improve and Develop the Bot: Analyzing command usage helps us understand which features are most popular and what we can improve.
  • ⬩ To Provide Technical Support: In case of an issue, logs can help us diagnose and resolve the error.
  • ⬩ To Ensure Security: We monitor Bot usage to detect and prevent spam, abuse, or other actions that violate our Terms of Service.

3. Data Sharing and Storage

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • ⬩ We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.
  • ⬩ The Bot's data is stored on secure servers provided by our hosting provider [Name of Hosting Provider, e.g., KEYHOST]. We choose partners who guarantee high standards of security.
  • ⬩ We may disclose your information only if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You have the right to control your data. We guarantee you the following rights:

  • ⬩ The Right to Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • ⬩ The Right to Rectification: You can request to have inaccurate or incomplete data corrected.
  • ⬩ The Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You can request the deletion of your data from our systems. In most cases, removing the Bot from your server will delete the associated configuration data. For a complete user data deletion request, please contact us.

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any significant changes via our support server or by updating the date at the top of this page.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect the functionality of our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at: omnitrixcenter@gmail.com or join our support server on Discord: Discord Support Server.
